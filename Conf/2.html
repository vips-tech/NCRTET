About NCRTET

The NCRTET - 2025 Hybrid Conference (online and offline) is a premier platform for exploring innovations in smart technologies, communication, and robotics. It brings together researchers, professionals, and students to share the latest advancements and network with leading experts. Featuring technical sessions, tutorials, and panels, the conference highlights key areas like high-speed data communication, energy-efficient wireless devices, and GPS-based vehicle positioning. Authors are invited to submit papers, as the event seeks to drive groundbreaking research in telecommunication. Join us to exchange ideas and advance the future of smart technologies.
